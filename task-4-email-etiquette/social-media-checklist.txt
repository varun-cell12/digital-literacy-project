# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's

* Use your real name and a professional profile photo
* Keep your account privacy settings secure
* Share informative and positive content
* Respect others and communicate politely
* Think carefully before posting anything

## ❌ Don'ts

* Do not share personal information like passwords, OTPs, or bank details
* Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
* Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
* Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
* Do not spread fake news or unverified information
