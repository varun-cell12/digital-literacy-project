# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

* Never share your OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone
* Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive information
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails or messages
* Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Regularly update your apps and devices for security
* Do not respond to unknown calls asking for financial details
* Always use official apps for UPI and online payments
* Check transaction details carefully before confirming payments
* Report cybercrime immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
